Pallipalayam chicken

Firstly, the simplicity in the preparation method. There are no special masalas used here and only onions, red chillies are the main spices. Red chillies when sauteed in coconut oil release a beautiful aroma. The infused flavors of red chillies and onions make the Pallipalayam chicken popular all-over Tamilnadu. Next, the chicken is slow roasted and cooked completely with its own juice. Water is not used to cook the chicken because the onion moisture is enough to cook them.
